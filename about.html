Our Philosophy

We're in this business because we love animals and their humans. We believe in going the extra mile to keep you and your pets happy and healthy. We aim for loyal lifetime customers, not just a one-time transactions. We take care to use environmentally-friendly products for all of our services. We choose shampoos and other products that are not only safer for the environment, but safer for your pets health, and your health. We are commited to creating a safe, caring environment that brings out the best in you, your pets, and our team.
